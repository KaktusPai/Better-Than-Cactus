# Heal Golden Carrots

Eating a golden carrot heals the player a little (small instant heal on consume).

## How it works
- The `eat_golden_carrot` advancement triggers on consuming a golden carrot and
  rewards the `heal_golden_carrot` function.
- `load` is wired through `data/minecraft/tags/function/load.json`.
